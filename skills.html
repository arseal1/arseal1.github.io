Skills

Armor and Weapon skills are critical in determining how effective your build is. There are many different skills with unique benefits and applications, and it can be difficult to pick and choose which ones to incorporate. The tables below list relevant skills grouped by priority level.

Stats shown based on max skill level.

Stat Breakdown

  • Attack: Raw attack value, multiplied by factors such as critical hits and motion values.

  • Affinity: Critical hit chance.

  • Element: Elemental damage, dealt in addition to raw based on elemental hitzones.

High Priority

Skill Name Type Description Max Level Stats
Agitator Armor Skill Increases attack power and affinity when large monsters become enraged. 5 Attack +20 and Affinity +15% while active.
Coalescence Armor Skill Temporarily increases elemental attack power and status effects after recovering from blights or abnormal status. 3 Element +15% while active.
Maximum Might Armor Skill Increases affinity if stamina is kept full for a period of time. 3 Affinity +30% while active.
Burst Armor Skill Continuously landing hits gradually increases attack and elemental attack. (Amount increased depends on weapon.) 5 Attack +18 and Element +140 while active.
Razor Sharp Weapon Skill Prevents your weapon from losing sharpness. 3 50% chance not to consume sharpness on hit.
Element Attack (Fire, Water, ect.) Weapon Skill Increases water element attack power. (Elemental attack power has a maximum limit.) 3 Element +20% with a bonus +60.
Critical Boost Weapon Skill Increases the damage of critical hits. 5 Critical hit damage +40%.

Lower Priority/Optional

Skill Name Type Description Max Level Stats
Antivirus Armor Skill Once infected, makes it easier to overcome the Frenzy and increases affinity if cured.Strong, but situational. 3 Greatly increases recovery rate after contracting the Frenzy. Affinity +15% after recovery.
Weakness Exploit Armor Skill Increases the affinity of attacks that exploit a monster's weak points and wounds. 5 Affinity +30% on weak points, additional +20% on wounds.

Offensive Guard Weapon Skill Temporarily increases attack power after executing a perfectly-timed guard. 3 Attack +15% while active.
Critical Eye Weapon Skill Increases affinity. 5 Affinity +20%
Load Shells Weapon Skill Improves reloading, and increases the gunlance's shell capacity and charge blade's phial capacity. 2 Phial reloading speed increased, all phials filled on yellow gauge, overcharged on red.
Rapid Morph Weapon Skill Increases switch speed and power for switch axes and charge blades. 3 Speeds up and adds +20% attack during morph animations.
Focus Weapon Skill Increases the fill rate for weapons with gauges and the charge rate for weapons with charge attacks. 3 Speeds up charged sword attacks and gauge fills faster.

Set Bonuses

Armor pieces from the same set often provide very useful benefits when two or more pieces are worn at a time. These are typically more prevalent in endgame buildcrafting, as the best bonuses come from late-game monsters.
